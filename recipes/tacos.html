Tacos

These tasty circles fold right up so you can cram em down yer mouth

Tacos are the unofficial national sandwich of mexico.

A taco is just a tortilla with some stuff on it. Topping choices are endless, but today we're making meat tacos.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas (dont be a gringo, use corn)
  • Carne asada
  • Diced cebolla
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Your hottest hot sauce

Steps

  1. Grill up that meat
  2. Chop up them veggies and mix em together
  3. Warm up your tortillas
  4. Place your meat on the tacos
  5. Top with hot sauce and the veggie mix
  6. Pop those suckers in yer boca
