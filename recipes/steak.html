Steak

Just a piece of cow

Steak is a simple and nutritious meal that will fill you up.

All you need is a cut of that cow and a grill and you've got yourself a meal.

Ingredients

  • A slab of meat, the bigger the better
  • Salt n pepper

Steps

  1. Salt and pepper your meat hunk generously
  2. Grill up that meat, rare to medium rare, no more, no less
  3. Take it off the grill and let is rest
