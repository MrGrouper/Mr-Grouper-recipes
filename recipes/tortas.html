Tortas

The spanish word for sandwich is, you guessed it, "Torta"

Tortas are the official national sandwich of mexico. Its essentially a sandwich with mexican ingredients

Much like tacos, topping choices are endless, but today we're making meat tortas.

Ingredients

  • Bread, typically a bollilo (i think)
  • Carne asada
  • Pickled cebolla (Recipe coming soon!)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayo
  • Your hottest hot sauce

Steps

  1. Grill up that meat
  2. Chop up them veggies
  3. Toast your bread and slice in half
  4. Place your meat on the bread
  5. Mayo your bread
  6. Place meat and veggies and hot sauce on your bread and slice that guy in half
  7. Pop that sucker in yer boca
